Beginner Tips

These are general gameplay tips.

Focus on last-hitting minions and staying alive. Gold and experience lead to stronger items and levels.

Place wards around objectives and common gank paths. Vision reduces surprise deaths and enables smarter plays.

If an enemy misses an important ability, that’s your opening. If your escape is down, play safer.
