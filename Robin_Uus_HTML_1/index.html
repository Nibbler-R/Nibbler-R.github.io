1944

Director: Elmo Nüganen

Genre: Drama

Year Released: 2015

Awards: Best Foreign Language Film
Fun Fact: Based on real historical events

1944 is a gripping Estonian war drama about loyalty, identity, and survival.
The film depicts the harsh reality faced by Estonian soldiers forced to fight on opposite sides during World War II.
War divides a nation, but humanity unites its people—this is the central message of the movie.
The soldiers discover courage and compassion amidst the chaos of war.
